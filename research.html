Research Areas

My research is mainly in graph theory and discrete mathematics, with particular interest in graph transformations, graph operators, switching operations, and resistance distances.

Current Research Topics

  • Partition switching of simple graphs
  • Self-switching graphs and Seidel switching
  • Graph folding and generalized Petersen graphs
  • Graph operators arising from cycles and subgraphs
  • Resistance distance formulas in ladder, fan, and wheel graphs
